Palawan

Welcome to the fifth largest island in the Philippines, Palawan! See the majestic seascapes and beautiful landscapes. Vist and see the unmatched opportunities for adventure, culture, and nature.

Baguio

Beautiful Baguio, also known as "The City Of Pines"! This famous tourist spot is best known for its breathtaking hillside view, enormous pine trees, and its sought after green park spaces.

Boracay

One of the top islands of the Philippines, Boracay! This beach is known for its beautiful white sand, clear waters, and stunning nature view.
